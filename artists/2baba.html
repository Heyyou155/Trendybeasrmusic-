2Baba Biography: Innocent Ujah Idibia (born in Jos, Plateau State, Nigeria),
known by his stage name 2Baba, is a Nigerian singer,songwriter, record producer, entrepreneur, philanthropist and activist. Prior to July 2014, he went by the stage name 2face Idibia.He is one of the most decorated and successful Afro pop artists in Africa, and is also one of the most bankable performing music artistes in Africa.
