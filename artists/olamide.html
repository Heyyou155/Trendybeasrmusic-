Names: Olamide Adedeji

Also Known As: Olamide

Record Label: ‎YBNL Nation

Genre: Afro beatz

Olamide Biography: Born on 15 March 1989 in Bariga, Lagos. Olamide Adedeji professionally known as Olamide is a Nigerian rapper, singer and songwriter. He records mostly in Yoruba and English. His debut studio album Rapsodi was released In 2011 while signed to Coded Tunes. The album YBNL was released under his label imprint YBNL Nation. He founded YBNL Nation record label and event management company in 2012.
